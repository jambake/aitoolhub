Speechify Review

★★★★★

Transform any text into studio-quality audio with AI

What is Speechify?

Speechify is a powerful AI text-to-speech platform that converts any written content into natural-sounding audio. Whether you have PDFs, articles, documents, or web pages, Speechify can read them aloud in over 30 languages with more than 100 high-quality voices.

Key Features

  • 100+ Natural Voices: Choose from celebrity voices like Gwyneth Paltrow and Snoop Dogg, or professional narration voices
  • Multi-Language Support: 30+ languages with accent options
  • Chrome Extension: Listen to any web article with one click
  • OCR Technology: Scan and read physical documents
  • Speed Control: Adjust playback speed from 0.5x to 3x
  • SDK for Developers: Integrate Speechify into your own apps

Our Experience

We tested Speechify extensively and were impressed by the audio quality. The voices sound remarkably natural, and the ability to speed up playback without distorting pronunciation is a game-changer for consuming content faster.

The Chrome extension is particularly useful - you can click a button on any article and have it read aloud immediately. Great for multitasking or consuming content while exercising or commuting.

Pros & Cons

✅ Pros

  • Exceptional voice quality
  • Celebrity voices available
  • Great Chrome extension
  • OCR for physical documents
  • Mobile apps for iOS and Android

❌ Cons

  • Free tier is limited
  • Some premium voices cost extra
  • Occasional processing delays

Verdict

Speechify is the best AI text-to-speech tool we've tested. Whether you're a student, professional, or content creator, the ability to convert any text to audio opens up new possibilities for consuming content. The premium pricing is worth it for unlimited access and all features.
