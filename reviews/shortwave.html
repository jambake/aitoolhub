What is Shortwave?

Shortwave is an AI-powered email client designed for professionals who want to reclaim their time from email management. It uses advanced AI to summarize threads, draft responses, and automatically organize your inbox.

Key Features

  • AI Email Summaries: Get instant summaries of long email threads and Newsletters
  • Smart Composing: AI helps you write emails faster with context-aware suggestions
  • Auto-Organization: Automatically sorts emails into smart folders based on content
  • Newsletter Digest: Summarize multiple newsletters into one daily digest
  • Quick Replies: Generate professional responses with one click
  • Search Enhancement: Find any email instantly with natural language search

Who It's For

  • Professionals drowning in email
  • Founders and executives who need email efficiency
  • Anyone who subscribes to many newsletters
  • Teams wanting better email organization
  • Remote workers managing multiple inboxes

Pricing

Shortwave offers a free tier with basic AI features. Pro plans start at $9/month for unlimited AI summaries and advanced features. Business plans with team features start at $15/user/month.

Pros & Cons

✅ Pros

  • Dramatically reduces email reading time
  • Excellent newsletter digestion feature
  • Clean, modern interface
  • Strong privacy and security
  • Integrates with Gmail and Google Workspace

❌ Cons

  • Limited to Gmail/Google Workspace initially
  • AI features require paid plans for heavy use
  • Learning curve for new interface
  • Occasional sync delays

Our Verdict

Shortwave is a game-changer for email management. The AI summaries alone can save you 30+ minutes daily by quickly digesting long threads and newsletters. It's particularly valuable for anyone who receives high volumes of email or subscribes to multiple newsletters. The integration with Gmail makes it easy to adopt. While the free tier is limited, the Pro plan is worth it for professionals. Highly recommended for anyone looking to modernize their email experience.
