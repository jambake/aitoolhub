What is Visily?

Visily is an AI-powered design tool that helps teams create wireframes and prototypes in seconds. Its standout feature is the ability to convert screenshots, hand-drawn sketches, or even rough ideas into editable designs automatically.

Key Features

  • AI Screenshot to Design: Upload any screenshot and Visily converts it into an editable design
  • Sketch to Design: Draw rough wireframes and let AI transform them into polished designs
  • Text to Design: Describe what you want and Visily generates wireframes
  • Collaboration: Real-time team collaboration with sharing and commenting
  • Component Library: Pre-built UI components and templates
  • Wireframing Mode: Quick, low-fidelity design for rapid prototyping

Pros

  • Revolutionary AI that dramatically speeds up design workflow
  • Perfect for rapid prototyping and ideation
  • Easy to use - no design experience required
  • Great for converting existing designs or competitor sites
  • Free tier available for individuals

Cons

  • Still a relatively new tool with ongoing improvements
  • Advanced features require paid plans
  • May not replace full design tools like Figma for complex projects

Pricing

Visily offers a free forever tier for individuals. Paid plans start at $12/month for teams, with additional AI credits and collaboration features.

Best For

  • Product managers who need quick wireframes
  • Startup founders validating ideas
  • Developers who want to prototype before coding
  • UX designers looking to speed up ideation
