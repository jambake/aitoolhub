What is Mem?

Mem is an AI-powered note-taking app that organizes your notes automatically using AI. Unlike traditional note-taking tools that rely on folders and manual organization, Mem uses AI to surface the right notes at the right time. It's designed to be a "second brain" that works for you.

Key Features

  • AI Auto-Organization: Notes are automatically tagged and connected to related content
  • Smart Search: Find anything instantly with AI-powered search
  • Meeting Notes: Automatically generate notes from meetings
  • Knowledge Base: Build a searchable knowledge base over time
  • Cross-Platform: Works on web, iOS, and Android

Pros

  • No manual organization needed
  • Fast and intuitive interface
  • Powerful AI search
  • Great for meeting notes
  • Integrates with calendar

Cons

  • Limited customization options
  • Premium plans can be pricey
  • Learning curve for AI-first workflow

Pricing

Free plan available. Pro plan starts at $10/month. Business plans available for teams.

Verdict

Mem is perfect for anyone who wants AI to handle the organization of their notes. If you're tired of manually sorting and filing notes, Mem's AI-first approach is a game-changer. It's especially useful for professionals who take a lot of meeting notes.
