What is Veed.io?

Veed.io is an AI-powered online video editing platform that makes professional video creation accessible to everyone. Whether you're a content creator, marketer, or educator, Veed.io provides intuitive tools powered by artificial intelligence to streamline your video production workflow. The platform runs entirely in your browser, meaning no software installation is required.

Key Features

🎬 Auto Subtitle Generator

Automatically generate accurate subtitles from your video's audio using AI speech recognition. Supports 100+ languages.

🎨 AI Video Editor

Smart editing tools that understand context and suggest appropriate cuts, transitions, and effects.

📝 Auto Translate

Instantly translate your subtitles and video content into 100+ languages with one click.

🎭 AI Avatar

Create videos with AI-generated virtual presenters and avatars without needing to appear on camera.

✂️ Video Background Remover

Remove backgrounds from any video instantly using AI - no green screen required.

🎵 AI Voiceover

Generate natural-sounding voiceovers from text in a variety of accents and languages.
