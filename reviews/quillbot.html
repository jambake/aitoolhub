What is QuillBot?

QuillBot is an AI-powered writing assistant that helps users paraphrase, rewrite, and improve their written content. Founded in 2017, it has become one of the most popular writing tools used by students, professionals, and content creators worldwide.

Key Features

  • Paraphraser: 8 different modes including Standard, Fluency, Formal, Creative, and more
  • Grammar Checker: Advanced grammar and punctuation correction
  • Plagiarism Checker: Ensure content originality
  • Summarizer: Condense long documents into key points
  • Citation Generator: Create citations in APA, MLA, and Chicago formats
  • Translator: Translate text between 30+ languages
  • Co-writer: AI-powered writing suggestions and improvements

Our Verdict

QuillBot is an excellent tool for anyone who needs help with writing, from students working on papers to professionals crafting emails. The paraphrasing feature is particularly strong, offering multiple modes to suit different writing styles and purposes.

While the free version is somewhat limited, the Premium plan offers excellent value at under $10/month. The added features like plagiarism checking and citation generation make it particularly valuable for academic use.
