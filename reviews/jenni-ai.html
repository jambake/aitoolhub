What is Jenni AI?

Jenni AI is an AI-powered academic writing assistant designed specifically for students, researchers, and academics. With over 6 million users across universities worldwide, it helps users write research papers, essays, and academic documents with proper citations and sourced content.

Unlike general-purpose AI writing tools, Jenni AI focuses on academic integrity, citation management, and research-backed content generation. It integrates with Zotero and Mendeley, and can search through 200 million+ academic papers.

Key Features

📚 Academic Library Search

Search across 200M+ academic papers and import sources directly into your document with proper citations.

📝 AI Autocomplete

Smart AI suggests sentences grounded in real research papers, with automatic citations included.

🔄 Citation Manager

Automatically cite in APA, MLA, Harvard, or IEEE formats. Integrates with Zotero and Mendeley.

🔍 Plagiarism Checker

Built-in plagiarism detection ensures your work is original and properly attributed.

📖 PDF Analysis

Upload PDFs and let Jenni extract key information, summarize findings, and help you cite properly.

Essay outlines, literature reviews, abstracts, thesis statements, and more specialized modes.
