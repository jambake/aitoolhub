What is Zebracat?

Zebracat is an AI-powered video creation platform that lets you turn text prompts, blog URLs, scripts, or audio files into professional-quality videos in minutes. It's designed for marketers, content creators, and businesses who want to scale their video marketing without the traditional time and cost investment.

What sets Zebracat apart is its end-to-end approach: it handles strategy, creation, scheduling, and even learns from performance data to improve future videos.

Key Features

  • Text-to-Video: Enter a prompt or script and Zebracat generates a video automatically
  • URL-to-Video: Paste a blog post URL and it converts the content into a video
  • AI Avatars: Choose from human-like avatars to present your content
  • Voice Cloning: Clone your own voice for consistent brand presence
  • AI Script Writing: Generate engaging scripts that hook your audience
  • Auto Captioning: Add accurate captions to every video automatically
  • AI Editing: AI handles the heavy lifting with manual tweaks available
  • Auto-Scheduling: Schedule and post directly to social platforms

Verdict
