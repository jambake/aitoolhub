What is Clipdrop?

Clipdrop is an AI-powered image editing platform created by Stability AI (the makers of Stable Diffusion). It offers a suite of powerful tools for removing backgrounds, relighting images, upscaling, and creating stunning visual content in seconds.

Key Features

🧹 Remove Background

Instantly remove backgrounds from any image with AI precision. Perfect for product photos, portraits, and creative projects.

💡 Relight

Add realistic lighting effects to your images. Control light direction, intensity, and ambiance with simple controls.

🔍 Image Upscaler

Increase image resolution up to 4x without quality loss. Perfect for enlarging photos for print or web use.

✏️ Replace Background

Remove and replace backgrounds seamlessly. Describe what you want, and AI generates the perfect backdrop.

🎭 Stable Diffusion Integration

Access powerful AI generation tools directly. Create, edit, and enhance images with cutting-edge AI models.

📱 API Access

Integrate Clipdrop's AI capabilities into your own apps and workflows with their robust API.

How much does it cost?

Clipdrop offers a free tier with limited uses, plus paid plans:

  • Pro: $9.99/month - Unlimited credits
