What is Recraft AI?

Recraft AI is an advanced AI-powered design tool that excels at creating vector art, illustrations, and branded designs. Unlike traditional AI image generators, Recraft focuses on producing production-ready assets that can be directly used in marketing materials, social media, and product design.

Key Features

  • Vector Art Generation: Creates scalable vector graphics perfect for logos and branding
  • Illustration Styles: Multiple art styles including flat, 3D, and hand-drawn looks
  • Brand Consistency: Maintain consistent visual identity across all generated designs
  • Image Editing: Inpainting and outpainting capabilities for refining outputs
  • API Access: Developer-friendly API for integrating into workflows

Performance & Quality

Recraft AI delivers impressive results, particularly for vector-based outputs. The quality of illustrations matches premium design work, and the vector exports work seamlessly in tools like Adobe Illustrator and Figma. Text rendering has improved significantly, though it still occasionally produces artifacts.
