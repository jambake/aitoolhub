What is ChatPDF?

ChatPDF is an AI-powered tool that allows you to interact with PDF documents using natural language. Simply upload any PDF, and you can ask questions, get summaries, extract information, and analyze content without reading through the entire document.

Launched in 2023, ChatPDF has become essential for students, researchers, legal professionals, and anyone who works with PDF documents regularly.

Key Features

  • Instant Q&A: Ask questions in plain English and get precise answers
  • Multi-document Support: Chat with multiple PDFs simultaneously
  • Citation System: Answers include page references
  • Language Support: Works with PDFs in 50+ languages
  • Summary Generation: Get quick summaries of long documents
  • Cross-document Search: Find information across multiple PDFs

Pricing

ChatPDF offers a generous free tier:

  • Free: 3 PDFs per day, 120 pages per PDF
  • Plus ($5/month): 500 PDFs per day, 2000 pages per PDF
  • Pro ($15/month): Unlimited PDFs, 10,000+ pages per PDF

✅ Pros

❌ Cons
