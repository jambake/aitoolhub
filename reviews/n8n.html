What is n8n?

n8n (pronounced "n-eight-n") is a powerful open-source workflow automation platform that lets you connect apps, automate tasks, and build complex workflows without writing code. Unlike Zapier or Make, n8n offers a self-hosted option, giving you complete control over your data and workflows.

With over 400+ integrations and a visual workflow builder, n8n is perfect for developers, businesses, and power users who want flexibility and control over their automation.

Key Features

🔓 Open-Source & Self-Hosted: Run n8n on your own servers for complete data privacy and control. The community edition is free forever.

🎨 Visual Workflow Builder: Drag-and-drop interface makes building complex automations simple. No coding required for basic workflows.

🔗 400+ Integrations: Connect to almost any API or service - from Slack to Salesforce, GitHub to Google Sheets.

🤖 AI Workflow Builder: Built-in AI capabilities for building intelligent automation with LLMs and AI agents.

⚡ Webhooks & Triggers: Multiple trigger options including webhooks, schedules, and event-based triggers.

🔄 Data Transformation: Powerful JSON manipulation and data transformation capabilities built-in.

✅ Pros

  • Open-source with free self-hosted option
  • Complete data control (no third-party servers)
  • Highly flexible and customizable
  • Active community with 1000+ nodes
  • One-time payment option for self-hosted
  • Excellent for developers and technical users
  • Custom webhook endpoints included

❌ Cons

  • Steeper learning curve than Zapier
  • Self-hosted requires technical knowledge
  • Limited customer support for free tier
  • Some features only on cloud plans
  • Mobile app not as polished

Best For

n8n is ideal for developers, tech-savvy businesses, and anyone who needs complete control over their automation. It's perfect if you want to avoid monthly subscription costs through self-hosting, or need advanced customization that Zapier doesn't offer.

Small teams and startups especially benefit from the free self-hosted option, while larger enterprises can leverage the cloud plans with dedicated support.
