What is Uizard?

Uizard is an AI-powered design platform that lets you create stunning websites, web apps, and mobile apps without any design experience. Simply describe what you want in plain English, and Uizard's AI generates a complete, editable design in seconds. You can also transform screenshots, sketches, or wireframes into fully editable UI designs.

Key Features

  • Autodesigner: Generate complete UI designs from text prompts
  • Screenshot to Design: Convert any screenshot into an editable wireframe
  • Sketch to UI: Turn hand-drawn sketches into digital mockups
  • Wireframe Mode: Quickly create low-fidelity wireframes
  • AI Writer: Generate placeholder text for your designs
  • Drag-and-Drop Editor: Full-featured design editor with customization
  • Component Library: Pre-built UI components and templates
  • Figma Integration: Export designs to Figma seamlessly

Who It's For

  • Product managers who need quick prototypes
  • Startup founders building MVPs
  • Non-designers creating landing pages
  • UX designers seeking rapid ideation
  • Developers who want to visualize ideas quickly

Pricing

Uizard offers a free forever plan with limited AI generations. Paid plans start at $19/month for the Pro plan with unlimited AI generations and advanced features. Business and Enterprise plans available for teams.
