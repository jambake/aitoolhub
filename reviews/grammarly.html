What is Grammarly?

Grammarly is an AI-powered writing assistant that helps you write clearer, more polished content. Founded in 2009 and now used by over 30 million people daily, it checks grammar, spelling, punctuation, clarity, engagement, and delivery across virtually every platform where you type.

Whether you're writing emails, essays, social media posts, or professional documents, Grammarly's AI analyzes your text in real-time and provides suggestions to improve your writing instantly.

Key Features

  • Grammar & Spelling: Catches hundreds of grammar and spelling errors
  • Style Improvements: Suggests better word choices and sentence structures
  • Tone Detection: Analyzes if your writing sounds formal, casual, confident, or friendly
  • Clarity Checks: Rewrites confusing sentences for better readability
  • Plagiarism Detector: Checks if your content is original (Premium)
  • Browser Extension: Works across Gmail, Google Docs, Slack, and more
  • Microsoft Office Add-in: Integrates with Word and Outlook
  • Desktop Apps: Dedicated apps for Windows and Mac
