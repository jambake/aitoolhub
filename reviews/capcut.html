What is CapCut?

CapCut is a free, all-in-one video editor powered by AI that's taken the creator economy by storm. Originally a mobile app, it now offers a powerful web and desktop version that rivals professional editing software. With features like auto-captions, AI-powered background removal, smart motion tracking, and thousands of templates, CapCut makes video editing accessible to everyone.
