What is Perplexity?

Perplexity combines the power of AI with real-time web search. Instead of giving you links, it answers your questions directly - with citations so you can verify the information.

It's like having a research assistant who can instantly find and summarize information from across the web.

Key Features

  • Cited Answers - Every claim links to its source
  • Follow-up Questions - Deep dive into any topic
  • Different AI Models - GPT-4, Claude, and more
  • Image Search - Find and analyze images
  • Collections - Save and organize research

Pricing

  • Free - Limited searches per day
  • Pro - $20/month - Unlimited searches, all models
  • Pro Plus - $30/month - Priority, larger context

✅ Pros

  • Cited sources for every answer
  • Fast, accurate information
  • Great for research
  • Multiple AI models
  • Follow-up questions work well

❌ Cons

  • Can miss nuanced results
  • Less comprehensive than Google
  • Free tier is limited
  • Occasional hallucinated citations
