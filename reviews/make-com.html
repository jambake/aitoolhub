What is Make.com?

Make.com (formerly known as Integromat) is a powerful visual automation platform that allows users to connect apps and automate workflows without writing a single line of code. Unlike traditional automation tools, Make offers a drag-and-drop interface that makes complex integrations accessible to everyone—from marketers to entrepreneurs to small business owners.


With over 1,000+ built-in app integrations, Make enables you to create sophisticated automated workflows (called "scenarios") that connect your favorite tools and streamline your business processes. Whether you need to automatically sync data between CRM systems, create social media posts from RSS feeds, or automate customer support workflows, Make has you covered.

Key Features

🔗 1,000+ App Integrations

Connect to virtually any app including Google Workspace, Slack, Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, and hundreds more.

🎨 Visual Workflow Builder

Drag-and-drop interface makes building complex automations intuitive—no coding required.

🔄 Real-Time Sync

Keep your data synchronized across all connected apps in real-time with minimal latency.

⏱️ Scheduling Options

Run workflows on a schedule, instantly, or set up webhooks for event-triggered automation.

📊 Data Transformation

Built-in tools to parse, filter, aggregate, and manipulate data between app connections.

🔒 Enterprise Security

SOC 2 Type II certified with encryption at rest and in transit for secure automation.

Our Verdict

Make.com is an exceptional automation tool that bridges the gap between powerful enterprise automation and accessibility for non-technical users. The visual interface makes it incredibly easy to build complex workflows, while the extensive app library ensures you can connect virtually any tool in your stack.


The generous free tier allows you to test the platform thoroughly before committing to a paid plan. For small businesses and entrepreneurs, the $9/month Basic plan offers excellent value, while larger teams will find the Teams plan cost-effective compared to hiring a developer to build custom integrations.


Rating: 4.7/5.0 — Highly recommended for anyone looking to automate repetitive tasks and streamline their workflow.
