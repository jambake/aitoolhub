What is ElevenLabs?

ElevenLabs is an AI voice cloning and text-to-speech platform that creates ultra-realistic synthetic voices. Founded in 2022, it has become the industry leader in AI audio, used by content creators, authors, podcasters, and enterprises.

Their technology can clone a voice from a short audio sample and generate new speech in that voice with incredible accuracy. They also offer a library of pre-built voices across multiple languages.

Key Features

  • Voice Cloning: Create a digital copy of any voice from just 1-3 minutes of audio
  • Multi-language Support: Generate speech in 29 languages with native-quality accents
  • Voice Design: Create unique AI voices from scratch using parameters
  • Emotion Control: Adjust emotional tone - happiness, sadness, excitement
  • Stability & Clarity: Industry-leading audio quality with minimal artifacts
  • API Access: Integrate ElevenLabs into apps, games, and workflows
