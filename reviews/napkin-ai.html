What is Napkin AI?

Napkin AI is a visual storytelling platform that transforms your text, notes, and ideas into professional-quality diagrams, flowcharts, and infographics automatically. Whether you're drafting business proposals, creating presentation materials, or documenting processes, Napkin AI converts written content into eye-catching visuals in seconds.

No design skills required - just paste your text or describe what you need, and Napkin AI generates multiple visual options you can customize to match your brand.

Key Features

  • Text-to-Visual Conversion: Paste any text and instantly generate relevant diagrams
  • Multiple Visual Types: Flowcharts, org charts, timelines, mind maps, and more
  • Auto-Layout: Smart positioning and spacing for professional results
  • Brand Customization: Match colors, fonts, and styles to your brand identity
  • Export Options: Download as PNG, SVG, or PDF for any use case
  • Presentation Mode: Built-in presentation view for showcasing visuals
  • Collaboration: Share and edit visuals with your team in real-time

How It Works

Napkin AI uses advanced AI to understand the semantic meaning of your text and automatically selects the most appropriate visual representation:

  • Paste your text, bullet points, or describe your idea
  • AI analyzes the content and suggests relevant visual formats
  • Choose from multiple generated options
  • Customize colors, labels, and styling
  • Export or present directly from the platform

Pricing

Napkin AI offers a free tier with basic features, perfect for trying the platform and creating simple visuals. The Pro plan at $9/month unlocks unlimited exports, advanced customization, and higher resolution outputs. Team plans starting at $29/month include collaboration features and brand management.

Best For

Napkin AI is ideal for business professionals who need to create visuals quickly without design expertise. It's perfect for startup founders building pitch decks, managers creating process documentation, marketers developing content visuals, and educators preparing teaching materials. Anyone who communicates ideas visually will benefit from this tool.
