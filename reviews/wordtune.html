What is Wordtune?

Wordtune is an AI-powered writing assistant developed by AI21 Labs that helps you rewrite, summarize, and improve your writing. Unlike basic grammar checkers, Wordtune uses advanced language AI to understand context and offer multiple rewriting options to make your writing clearer, more engaging, and more professional.

Available as a browser extension (Chrome, Edge, Firefox), web editor, and integrations with Google Docs, Gmail, Slack, and more.

Key Features

  • Rewrite/Spin: Get 10+ alternative ways to rewrite any sentence
  • Summarizer: Condense long documents into key points
  • Tone Adjuster: Change tone (formal, casual, professional)
  • Expander & Shortener: Add detail or condense text
  • Grammar & Spelling: Built-in grammar correction
  • Multi-language Support: Works in 10 languages

Verdict

Rating: 4.5/5

Wordtune is an excellent writing companion for anyone who wants to improve their writing quickly. The browser extension makes it easy to use anywhere, and the rewrite suggestions are generally helpful. It's especially useful for non-native English speakers and professionals who need to write polished content regularly.

The free plan is great for casual use, but if you're writing professionally, the Premium plan is worth it for unlimited rewrites.
