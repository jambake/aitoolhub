Overview

ClickUp Brain is ClickUp's AI-powered productivity suite that serves as "Your Company's Brain." It combines multiplayer AI collaboration, a self-updating knowledge graph, and context-aware automation into a single platform. Unlike standalone AI assistants, ClickUp Brain integrates directly into your workflow, understanding your tasks, docs, and conversations in real-time.

Key Features

  • Company Brain2: Self-updating knowledge graph that learns from your workspace
  • Multiplayer AI: Threaded dialogue with shared, persistent context windows for teams
  • Context Engine: Auto-clusters entities into semantic knowledge graph with continuous re-indexing
  • Every AI Model: GPT, Claude Opus, Gemini, and more - one subscription for all frontier models
  • Deep Search: Searches your workspace, connected apps, and the web simultaneously
  • Ambient Intelligence: Surfaces relevant context and suggestions before you ask
  • Presentation Builder: Creates polished slides from prompts in minutes
  • Data Analysis: Parse files, cross-reference data, and generate charts on command

ClickUp Brain stands out because it doesn't just respond to prompts - it actively learns your organization's context. The AI sees your tasks, documents, and conversations in real-time, eliminating the need to provide lengthy briefs. It claims to be 85% cheaper than OpenAI & Claude while delivering quality ranked #1 in benchmarks.
