What is Opus AI?

Opus AI refers to Anthropic's Claude model family—specifically the Claude 3 Opus model, which represents the company's most powerful offering. Named after the highest tier in the Claude 3 model family, Opus stands as one of the most advanced AI assistants available in 2026.

Unlike faster AI models, Claude Opus emphasizes thoughtful, nuanced responses. It's designed for complex reasoning, long-form analysis, and tasks requiring deep contextual understanding.

Key Features

🧠 Advanced Reasoning

Superior logical thinking and step-by-step problem solving for complex tasks.

📚 Massive Context Window

Handle up to 200K tokens—equivalent to reading hundreds of pages in one go.

✍️ Superior Writing

Naturally flowing, well-structured prose for articles, emails, and creative content.

🔒 Safety-First Design

Built-in protections against harmful outputs and responsible AI guidelines.
