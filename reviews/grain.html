What is Grain AI?

Grain is an AI-powered meeting notes tool that automatically captures, organizes, and summarizes insights from your meetings. It integrates with popular video conferencing platforms to provide real-time transcription and AI-generated summaries, making it effortless to capture important decisions, action items, and customer feedback.

Key Features

🎙️ Auto Transcription

Real-time transcription of meetings with high accuracy across multiple speakers.

📝 Smart Summaries

AI-generated summaries that highlight key points, decisions, and action items.

🔗 Integrations

Seamless integration with Slack, Salesforce, Notion, and 50+ other tools.

🏷️ Auto Tags & Highlights

Automatically tags and highlights important moments, customer feedback, and decisions.

🔍 Search & Discovery

Search across all past meetings to find specific conversations and insights instantly.

📤 Shareable Clips

Extract and share specific video clips with timestamps for easy sharing.

Conclusion

Its strong transcription accuracy, smart summaries, and extensive integrations make it a valuable asset for customer-facing teams. While the pricing structure may not suit everyone, the time saved and improved team alignment often justify the investment.
