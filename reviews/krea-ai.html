What is Krea AI?

Krea AI is an innovative AI-powered creative platform that combines image generation, real-time rendering, and design tools. It's particularly known for its ability to understand and generate professional-quality visual content with an intuitive interface.

Key Features

  • Real-time Image Generation - Generate images as you type with instant feedback
  • AI Canvas - Infinite canvas for creative exploration
  • Sketch to Image - Convert rough sketches into polished images
  • Brand Consistency - Train AI on your brand aesthetics
  • Batch Generation - Create multiple variations at once
  • API Access - Integrate into your workflows

Use Cases

  • Graphic design and branding
  • Marketing visuals and social media
  • Product photography concepts
  • Interior design visualization
  • Concept art and illustration

Pricing

Krea AI offers a free tier with limited generations, while Pro plans start at $24/month for unlimited access and advanced features.
