What is Vidu AI?

Vidu is an AI video generation platform that creates high-quality videos from text prompts and images. Developed by Shenghao, Vidu offers realistic motion and cinematic quality that competes with Runway and Kling.

Key Features

  • Text-to-Video: Generate videos from text descriptions
  • Image-to-Video: Animate static images into dynamic videos
  • Multiple Styles: Supports realistic, anime, and cinematic styles
  • Reference Consistency: Maintain subject consistency across frames
  • Fast Generation: Quick turnaround times for video creation

Use Cases

  • Content creators needing quick video material
  • Marketing teams creating promotional content
  • Social media managers producing short-form content
  • Filmmakers exploring AI-assisted previsualization

Best For

Vidu is best for creators who want professional-quality AI videos without the complexity of traditional video editing. It's particularly strong for realistic motion and cinematic shots.

Pricing

Free tier available with limited generations. Paid plans start at $19/month for extended use.
