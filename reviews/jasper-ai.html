What is Jasper AI?

Jasper (formerly Jarvis) is one of the original AI writing assistants, designed specifically for marketers and content creators. It uses GPT-4 and other AI models to generate blog posts, ads, emails, social media content, and more. Jasper is known for its brand voice features and templates tailored for marketing workflows.

Key Features

50+ Templates

Pre-built templates for blogs, ads, product descriptions, emails, and social media posts.

Brand Voice

Train Jasper on your brand's style and tone for consistent output across all content.

Campaigns

Create multi-piece marketing campaigns with a single prompt - perfect for content series.

Surfer SEO Integration

Built-in SEO optimization with Surfer integration for ranking content.

Pro plan ($99/mo) includes unlimited words, priority support, and advanced features. Annual billing saves 20%.
