What is Kapwing?

Kapwing is an online video editing platform that combines traditional editing tools with powerful AI features. Founded in 2017, it's become especially popular among content creators, social media managers, and marketers who need to produce videos quickly without a steep learning curve.

Key Features

🎬 AI Video Generator

Turn text into videos automatically. Great for quick social posts and content repurposing.

✂️ Smart Edit

AI-powered editing that suggests cuts, adds subtitles, and removes silences automatically.

📝 Subtitle Generator

Automatically generate and translate subtitles in 70+ languages with 99% accuracy.

🎨 AI Art Generator

Create custom images and art directly in the editor using AI image generation.

🔊 AI Voiceover

Convert text to natural-sounding voiceovers in seconds without recording.

📱 Social Media Ready

Templates optimized for TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and more with auto-resize.
