What is Otter.ai?

Otter.ai is an AI-powered meeting assistant that provides real-time transcription, note-taking, and meeting insights. It captures meetings automatically, generates summaries, and lets you search through conversations instantly.

Key Features

  • Live Transcription: Real-time speech-to-text in 30+ languages
  • Meeting Summaries: AI-generated summaries with key decisions and action items
  • Speaker Identification: Recognizes and labels different speakers
  • Otter Chat: Ask questions about your meetings and get instant answers
  • Calendar Integration: Automatically joins and transcribes Zoom, Google Meet, Teams calls
  • CRM Integration: Push sales insights to Salesforce, HubSpot
  • AI Agents: Specialized agents for Sales, Education, Media, Recruiting

✅ Pros

  • Excellent transcription accuracy
  • Generates actionable meeting summaries
  • Works with Zoom, Google Meet, Teams
  • Free plan available with decent limits
  • Powerful search across all meetings
  • Mobile apps for on-the-go access

❌ Cons

  • Free tier limited to 300 minutes/month
  • Can struggle with heavy accents
  • Desktop app still has some bugs
  • Higher-tier plans get expensive

Verdict

Otter.ai is a must-have tool for professionals who attend many meetings. The AI-powered summaries alone save hours of time reviewing meeting notes. While the free tier is limited, the Pro plan at $10/month offers excellent value for individuals and small teams.
