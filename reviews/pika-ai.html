What is Pika AI?

Pika is an AI video generation platform that creates videos from text prompts, images, or by extending existing footage. Founded in 2023, it quickly became one of the most accessible AI video tools for creators.

Unlike complex video production tools, Pika makes video creation as simple as writing a prompt.

Key Features

  • Text to Video - Generate videos from text descriptions
  • Image to Video - Animate static images into motion
  • Video Extension - Extend any video by 4 seconds
  • Style Options - 3D animation, realistic, anime, and more
  • Fast Generation - Results in minutes, not hours
  • Edit Features - Modify elements within generated videos

Pricing

  • Free - 150 credits/month (generous free tier)
  • Pro - $15/month - 500 credits, priority generation
  • Ultra - $45/month - Unlimited, highest quality

✅ Pros

  • Very easy to use
  • Generous free tier
  • Fast video generation
  • Multiple style options
  • Great for social media content

❌ Cons

  • Limited to 4-second clips
  • Can struggle with complex motion
  • Quality varies by prompt
  • Sometimes produces artifacts
