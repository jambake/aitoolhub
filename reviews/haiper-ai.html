What is Haiper AI?

Haiper AI is an AI video generation platform that creates videos from text descriptions and images. Founded by former Google DeepMind researchers, it offers a free tier that makes AI video creation accessible to everyone. The platform specializes in smooth motion and realistic visuals.

Key Features

  • Text-to-Video: Generate videos from text prompts
  • Image-to-Video: Animate static images into dynamic videos
  • Video Extension: Extend existing videos up to 10 seconds
  • Style Options: Choose from various artistic styles
  • Free Tier: No credit card required to start

✅ Pros

  • Free tier available
  • Smooth, realistic motion
  • Easy to use interface
  • Video extension feature
  • No watermarks on free tier

❌ Cons

  • Limited to 4-second clips on free
  • Processing times can be long
  • Less control than competitors
  • Fewer advanced features

Alternatives

If Haiper AI doesn't meet your needs, consider these alternatives:

  • Runway: More advanced features, higher price point
  • Pika: Fast generation, great for social media
  • Kling AI: Chinese AI video with impressive results
  • Sora: OpenAI's video generation (waitlist)

Verdict

Haiper AI is an excellent starting point for creators who want to experiment with AI video without spending money. The free tier gives you enough to test the technology, while the Creator plan at $9.99/month is affordable for regular content creators. It's not as powerful as Runway or Kling, but it's a solid free option.
