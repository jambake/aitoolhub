What is Runway?

Runway is a leading AI-powered video generation and editing platform that has become a go-to tool for content creators, filmmakers, and designers. Known for its Gen-2 and Gen-3 video generation models, Runway enables users to create stunning video content from text prompts, images, and existing video footage.
