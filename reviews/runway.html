What is Runway ML?

Runway ML is a comprehensive AI video generation and editing platform that empowers creators to produce professional-quality video content using artificial intelligence. From text-to-video generation to advanced video editing tools, Runway has become the go-to platform for creators looking to scale their video production.

Founded in 2018 and headquartered in New York, Runway has partnered with major studios and is used by millions of creators worldwide for film, social media, and commercial content creation.
