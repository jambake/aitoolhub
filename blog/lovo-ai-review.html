What is Lovo AI?

Lovo AI is a powerful AI voice generator known for its extensive library of over 180 voices in more than 100 languages. It also includes built-in AI video editing capabilities, making it a versatile tool for global content creators.

While its voice quality may not always reach the peak realism of ElevenLabs, Lovo AI's vast language support and integrated video features make it a strong contender for international projects and diverse media needs.

  • Massive Voice & Language Library: Access over 180 voices across 100+ languages, ideal for localization.
  • AI Video Generation: Create basic videos or add voiceovers to existing footage within the platform.

Pricing

Lovo AI offers several plans:

  • Free — $0/month: Limited access to voices and features.
  • Starter — $25/month: Offers more voices, languages, and a higher generation limit.
  • Pro — $75/month: Full access to all features, including API and more voice creation.

While not always matching the top realism of others, its versatility and language breadth make it a valuable tool.
