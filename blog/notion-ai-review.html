What is Notion AI?

Notion AI is an AI-powered assistant integrated directly into Notion, the popular all-in-one workspace. It helps with writing, brainstorming, summarizing, and editing - all within your existing Notion workspace.

💰 Pricing: $10/month per user (or included with Notion Plus)

Key Features

  • AI Writing Assistant - Generate, edit, and improve content
  • Brainstorming - Get ideas and creative direction instantly
  • Summarization - Turn long notes into quick summaries
  • Extraction - Pull key info from meeting notes, documents
  • Grammar & Style - Real-time writing improvements

✅ Pros

  • Seamlessly integrated into Notion
  • Great for productivity workflows
  • Affordable at $10/month
  • Continuous improvements
  • Works across all Notion content

❌ Cons

  • Requires Notion subscription
  • Not as powerful as dedicated AI tools
  • Can feel limited for advanced writing
  • Learning curve for new users

Who Should Use It?

Notion AI is perfect for:

  • Already Notion users wanting AI boost
  • Teams using Notion for documentation
  • Students and researchers
  • Project managers and planners

Our Verdict

Notion AI is a solid choice if you're already in the Notion ecosystem. It's not going to replace ChatGPT for general AI tasks, but it excels at making your Notion workflow smarter and faster. At $10/month, it's reasonably priced for the productivity gains.
