Google Gemini Review - The Ultimate AI Assistant Test

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ June 3, 2026 AI Assistant

What is Google Gemini?

Google Gemini (formerly Bard) is Google's flagship AI assistant, powered by the Gemini family of models. It represents Google's answer to ChatGPT and Claude, offering native integration with Google's ecosystem of products.

Key Features

  • Multiple Model Tiers: Gemini Ultra (most capable), Gemini Pro (balanced), Gemini Nano (on-device)
  • Google Workspace Integration: Directly connects to Gmail, Docs, Drive, and more
  • Real-time Information: Access to Google Search for current data
  • Image Analysis: Upload and analyze images with detailed understanding
  • Code Execution: Write, debug, and execute code in multiple languages
  • Voice Mode: Conversational voice interactions (Gemini Live)

Performance

In our testing, Gemini Ultra outperforms GPT-4o on several benchmarks, particularly in:

  • Complex reasoning tasks
  • Multi-step problem solving
  • Mathematical computations
  • Code generation and debugging

The integration with Google Search makes it particularly strong for queries requiring current information.

Pros & Cons

✅ Pros

  • Excellent Google ecosystem integration
  • Real-time information access
  • Strong reasoning capabilities
  • Voice mode is impressive
  • Generous free tier

❌ Cons

  • Occasional hallucinations
  • Advanced features require paid plan
  • Less customizable than competitors

Verdict

Rating: 4.5/5

Google Gemini is a formidable AI assistant that directly competes with the best. The Google ecosystem integration alone makes it worth trying. For power users, the $19.99/month Advanced plan delivers genuine value with Ultra model access.
