What is Wordtune?

Wordtune is an AI writing assistant that focuses on rewriting and improving your existing text. Unlike tools that generate content from scratch, Wordtune makes your writing clearer, more formal, or more casual — you choose the tone.

It's like having a writing coach that suggests improvements in real-time. Available as a browser extension, it works where you write — emails, documents, social media.

Consider it if you write a lot and need quick tone adjustments without switching contexts.
