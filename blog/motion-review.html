What is Motion?

Motion is an AI-powered scheduling app that automatically plans your day based on your tasks, meetings, and priorities. It acts as your personal calendar manager, finding the best time for everything on your plate.

💰 Pricing: $19/month (Pro), $39/month (Team)

Key Features

  • Auto-Scheduling - AI automatically schedules tasks around meetings
  • Project Management - Task lists with deadlines and priorities
  • Meeting Scheduler - Find optimal meeting times instantly
  • Calendar Sync - Works with Google Calendar, Outlook, Apple Calendar
  • Team Coordination - See team's availability at a glance

✅ Pros

  • Intelligent priority management
  • Great for busy professionals
  • Seamless calendar integration
  • Team features are powerful

❌ Cons

Who Should Use It?

Our Verdict

Motion is a game-changer if you have a complex schedule. The AI does what most of us wish we had - a personal assistant managing our time. At $19/month, it's an investment, but the time saved is worth it for the right person.
