What is Motion?

Motion is an intelligent project management and scheduling platform that leverages AI to automate the planning of your tasks and meetings. Designed to combat the cognitive load of manual scheduling, Motion intelligently organizes your calendar and to-do list, finding the optimal time for every task based on priorities, deadlines, and availability.

It aims to eliminate the need for constant rescheduling and manual planning, particularly benefiting busy professionals, teams, and those managing complex projects. Motion integrates task management, calendar scheduling, and team collaboration into a single, AI-driven interface.

Key Features

  • Intelligent Task Scheduling: AI automatically finds optimal time slots for tasks in your calendar, considering deadlines and priorities.
  • Meeting Management: Facilitates scheduling meetings by finding available times for all attendees and automatically adding them to calendars.
  • Project Planning: Break down large projects into manageable tasks with dependencies and deadlines.
  • Calendar Integration: Syncs with Google Calendar, Outlook Calendar, and Apple Calendar.
  • Team Collaboration: Assign tasks, share projects, and manage team workloads.

AI Scheduling Engine

Motion's core strength lies in its AI scheduling engine:

  • Dynamic Rescheduling: If a meeting runs over or a new urgent task appears, Motion automatically adjusts the schedule to accommodate changes.
  • Prioritization Engine: Tasks are automatically prioritized and scheduled based on deadlines and user-defined importance.
  • Workload Balancing: Distributes tasks across team members and time slots to prevent overloading.
  • Focus Time Blocking: Automatically schedules blocks of time for focused work on important tasks.

Pricing

Motion offers tiered plans:

  • Individual — $19/month (billed annually): For individuals needing automated task and meeting scheduling.
  • Team — $30/user/month (billed annually): Includes all individual features plus team collaboration and management tools.

✅ Pros

  • Revolutionary automation of scheduling and task planning.
  • Significantly reduces time spent on manual organization.
  • Effective for managing complex projects and team workloads.

❌ Cons

Final Verdict

Motion is a powerful AI solution for anyone overwhelmed by scheduling and task management. Its ability to intelligently plan your day and automate meeting coordination is a true game-changer, freeing up significant mental energy. While the cost is a factor, the time savings and increased productivity make it a worthwhile investment for individuals and teams committed to maximizing their efficiency.
