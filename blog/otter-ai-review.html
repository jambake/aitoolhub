What is Otter.ai?

Otter.ai is an AI-powered meeting assistant that provides real-time transcription, summarization, and collaboration features. It's designed to capture and organize information from meetings, interviews, and conversations automatically.

Try Otter.ai Free

Key Features

  • Real-time Transcription: Live transcription as you speak
  • AI Summaries: Auto-generated meeting summaries
  • Speaker Identification: Recognizes different speakers
  • Calendar Integration: Connects with Google Calendar, Outlook
  • Collaboration: Share transcripts with team members
  • Search: Search through all your past conversations
  • Mobile App: Record and transcribe on the go

Our Experience

We tested Otter.ai across several meetings and found the transcription accuracy to be impressive, especially in English. The AI summary feature saves significant time reviewing meeting notes. The ability to highlight and annotate during meetings is particularly useful for follow-up action items.

The calendar integration makes it effortless to automatically join and record meetings. Overall, it's become an essential tool for our team productivity.
