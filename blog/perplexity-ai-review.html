🔍 Perplexity AI Review 2026

⭐ 4.5/5

What is Perplexity AI?

Perplexity is an AI-powered search engine that provides direct, cited answers to your questions rather than just listing links. It combines the best of traditional search with AI conversational interfaces.

💰 Pricing: FREE (limited) with Pro at $20/month

Key Features

  • Conversational Answers - Get human-like responses to complex questions
  • Source Citations - Every answer links back to original sources
  • Thread Follow-up - Ask follow-up questions within a search context
  • Multiple AI Models - Switch between GPT, Claude, and Perplexity's own models
  • Image & File Upload - Analyze images and documents directly
  • Research Mode - Deep dive into topics with cited sources

✅ Pros

  • Answers are comprehensive and cited
  • Great for research and learning
  • Free tier is surprisingly capable
  • Fast and responsive interface
  • Multi-model switching is excellent
  • Perfect for fact-checking

❌ Cons

  • Can hallucinate occasionally
  • Pro version is pricey at $20/month
  • Not as good for shopping/product searches
  • Learning curve for advanced features

Who Should Use It?

  • Researchers needing quick literature reviews
  • Students researching assignments
  • Professionals doing competitive analysis
  • Anyone tired of clicking through search results

Our Verdict

Perplexity has become my go-to for research and information gathering. The ability to get cited answers and then dive deeper with follow-up questions makes it invaluable. The free tier is enough for most casual use, but the Pro version is worth it if you use it daily.
