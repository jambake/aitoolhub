Vidu AI Review - Next-Gen AI Video Generator

Create stunning videos from text prompts in under 30 seconds

What is Vidu AI?

Vidu AI is an advanced AI video generation platform that transforms text prompts and images into high-quality videos. Developed in China, Vidu has quickly become one of the fastest AI video tools available, with rendering times under 30 seconds for many outputs.

Key Features

  • Text-to-Video: Generate videos from simple text descriptions
  • Image-to-Video: Animate static images into dynamic videos
  • Fast Rendering: Videos ready in under 30 seconds
  • Multiple Styles: Support for anime, realistic, and artistic styles
  • 4K Output: High-resolution video generation
  • Camera Controls: Pan, zoom, and other camera movements

Pricing

Vidu offers a freemium model:

  • Free Tier: Limited credits for testing
  • Pro Plan: Starting at $19/month for more credits
  • Business: Custom pricing for teams

✅ Pros

  • Extremely fast rendering
  • User-friendly interface
  • High-quality output
  • Affordable pricing
  • Multiple video styles

❌ Cons

  • Limited facial animation
  • Occasional artifacts
  • English interface sometimes buggy
