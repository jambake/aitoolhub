Manus AI Review 2026

Last updated: June 7, 2026

What is Manus AI?

Manus AI is an autonomous generalist AI agent that goes beyond answering questions to actually executing tasks, automating workflows, and extending your human reach. Developed by Chinese AI startup Monica, Manus made waves in early 2025 as a viral hit and was recently acquired by Meta for $2 billion.

Key Features

  • Autonomous Task Execution: Completes complex tasks from a single prompt without constant guidance
  • Browser Control: Navigates websites, fills forms, and performs web actions autonomously
  • Code Execution: Writes and runs code to build apps, analyze data, and solve problems
  • My Computer Feature: Controls your local files through a desktop application
  • Multi-domain Capability: Handles research, coding, content creation, and more

How It Compares to Competitors

Manus AI stands out from other AI agents like OpenAI Operator and Claude Computer Use:

  • vs OpenAI Operator: Manus is more autonomous and handles a wider range of tasks
  • vs Claude Computer Use: Manus offers better browser automation and file handling
  • vs Devin: Manus is more general-purpose, while Devin focuses on coding

Pricing

Manus AI offers a credit-based pricing system. New users receive free credits to try the service. Paid plans start at $19/month for casual users, with higher tiers for power users requiring more credits and faster processing.

Our Verdict

Manus AI represents a significant leap in AI agent capabilities. Its ability to autonomously execute complex workflows makes it invaluable for professionals and businesses. While it requires some learning curve, the time savings and productivity gains are substantial.
