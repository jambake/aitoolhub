What is Todoist?

Todoist is a widely-used task management application celebrated for its simplicity, speed, and cross-platform availability. It allows individuals and teams to capture, organize, and prioritize tasks effectively. Recently, Todoist has begun integrating AI features to enhance task management capabilities.

While its core strength remains straightforward task handling, the addition of AI aims to streamline scheduling, offer productivity insights, and improve natural language input for task creation, making it smarter and more intuitive.

Key Features

  • Natural Language Input: Type tasks like "Schedule meeting with team tomorrow at 3pm" and Todoist automatically sets the due date and time.
  • Recurring Due Dates: Set tasks to repeat daily, weekly, monthly, or using custom natural language phrases (e.g., "every other Tuesday").
  • Projects & Sub-tasks: Organize tasks into projects and break down larger tasks into smaller sub-tasks.
  • Priority Levels: Assign priority levels (P1 to P4) to ensure focus on what matters most.
  • Cross-Platform Sync: Seamlessly syncs across web, desktop, and mobile apps.

AI Integration

Todoist's AI features, while nascent, add significant value:

  • Smart Scheduling Suggestions: AI can suggest optimal times and dates for tasks based on your existing schedule and priorities.
  • Productivity Insights: The Karma system (in Pro plans) tracks your task completion and provides gamified feedback on your productivity habits. AI enhances these insights over time.
  • AI-Assisted Task Creation: Generate task descriptions or break down complex tasks into actionable sub-tasks using AI prompts.

Pricing

Todoist follows a freemium model:

  • Free — $0/month: Includes core task management features, up to 5 active projects.
  • Pro — $12/month (or $48/year): Unlimited projects, advanced filters, reminders, file uploads, and AI insights.
  • Business — $6/user/month (billed annually): For teams, including team folders and admin controls.

✅ Pros

  • Extremely simple and intuitive to use.
  • Fast and reliable cross-platform synchronization.
  • Natural language input for task creation is a major time-saver.
  • Robust free tier suitable for many users.
  • AI features enhance productivity tracking and suggestions.

❌ Cons

  • AI features are more focused on insights and suggestions rather than generative tasks.
  • Pro plan required for full AI benefits and unlimited projects.
  • Less suitable for complex project management involving deep dependencies or Gantt charts compared to dedicated PM software.

Final Verdict

Todoist remains a top-tier task manager, enhanced by smart AI features. Its simplicity and speed make it ideal for managing daily tasks and personal projects. The AI integration adds a layer of intelligence for scheduling and insights, making it more powerful without sacrificing ease of use. For individuals and small teams focused on clear task lists, Todoist is an excellent choice.
