What is Speechify?

Speechify is a powerful text-to-speech (TTS) platform that converts any written content into high-quality audio. Whether you're a student, professional, or content creator, Speechify makes it easy to consume content on the go.

Key Features

  • Natural Voices: Over 100 AI voices that sound human
  • Multi-language Support: 30+ languages available
  • Speed Control: Adjust playback speed from 0.5x to 3x
  • Chrome Extension: Listen to any web article
  • OCR Technology: Scan physical books and documents
  • Audiobook Creation: Convert PDFs, ePubs, and more

✅ Pros

  • Exceptionally natural voice quality
  • Great for learning and accessibility
  • Useful Chrome extension
  • Perfect for audiobooks and podcasts
  • Helps with proofreading written content

❌ Cons

  • Free version has limitations
  • Premium voices require paid plan
  • Some languages sound robotic

Pricing

Speechify offers a free tier with basic features. Premium plans start at $139/year for unlimited access to all voices and features. The Studio plan ($239/year) includes advanced AI dubbing and voice cloning.

Verdict

Speechify is an excellent tool for anyone who wants to consume written content as audio. It's particularly useful for students, professionals with reading difficulties, or anyone who prefers listening to reading. The free version is a great way to test it out, but the premium voices are worth the investment.
